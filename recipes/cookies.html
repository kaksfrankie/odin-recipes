The Ultimate chocolate cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • Coarsely chopped nuts
  • Sweetened chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Heat oven to 375Â°F. In small bowl, mix flour, baking soda and salt; set aside.

  2. Step 2

    3. In large bowl, beat softened butter and sugars with electric mixer on medium speed, or mix with spoon about 1 minute or until fluffy, scraping side of bowl occasionally.

  3. Step 3

    4. Beat in egg and vanilla until smooth. Stir in flour mixture just until blended (dough will be stiff). Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.

  4. Step 4

    5. Onto ungreased cookie sheets, drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart.

  5. Step 5

    6. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light brown (centers will be soft). Cool 2 minutes; remove from cookie sheet to cooling rack. Cool completely, about 30 minutes. Store covered in airtight container.
